How It Works

Phase 1: Baselines

Select a Pacific nation to establish geographic and demographic context before diving into the data.

Phase 2: Thematic Chapters

Data is organized seamlessly into three chapters (Agro-Ecosystems, Ocean Health, and Socio-Economics) with integrated regional benchmarking built directly into the visualizations.

Phase 3: Strategic Action

Transition from analysis to action. Review localized, data-driven adaptation steps powered by targeted context synthesis.
