The Challenge

Pacific Islands & Territories are uniquely vulnerable to the impacts of climate change. From rising sea surface temperatures disrupting local marine ecosystems to shifting weather patterns threatening agricultural yields, the consequences are multi-faceted.

One primary obstacle for policymakers and stakeholders is making sense of open source data to better understand the impact. There is an urgent need to translate abstract data into tangible, localized narratives that clearly illustrate vulnerabilities and pathways to resilience.

This application is built to answer that challenge — pairing open data visualization with storytelling to bridge the gap between raw datasets and public comprehension of climate insights, using open source technology (FastAPI, Jinja2, Plotly).
