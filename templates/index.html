Understanding the Climate Change Crisis in the Pacific Islands and Territories

The theme of the 2026 Pacific Dataviz Challenge is "Climate Change". Pacific Islands & Territories are uniquely vulnerable to the impacts of climate change. From rising sea surface temperatures disrupting local marine ecosystems to shifting weather patterns threatening agricultural yields, the consequences are multi-faceted.

One primary obstacle for policymakers and stakeholders is making sense of open source data to better understand the climate impact. There is an urgent need to translate abstract data into tangible, localized narratives that clearly illustrate vulnerabilities and pathways to resilience.

This application is built to bridge the gap between raw datasets and public comprehension of climate change. It transforms fragmented data into a seamless narrative across {{ territory_count }} Pacific countries and territories.

How It Works

Selection

Themes
